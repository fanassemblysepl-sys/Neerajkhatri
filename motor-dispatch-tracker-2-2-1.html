ℹ️ How This Works

1. Generate a barcode for each new motor and print/stick the label on it (before dispatch).
2. When the motor is actually dispatched, scan its barcode — the dispatch date is recorded automatically.
3. Scan the same barcode again anytime and its dispatch date shows up instantly.
